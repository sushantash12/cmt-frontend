With this application you can manage your vehicles and appointments. To get started:
  • You can click on the hamburger icon on the top left to open the navigational menu.
  • Here you will find different pages you can proceed with
  • If you click on My Appointments, you can see a list of consolidated appointments you made for your vehicles.
  • You can also create/cancel an appointment from here.
  • Click on My Vehicles to manage your vehicles
  • Here you can add a new Vehicle and edit/delete a vehicle if you want to.
  • You can also see your Vehicle Maintenance Records from here
  • In case you want to come back to this page again, clicking on the Home button will do.
With this application you can manage service providers and appointments. To get started:
  • You can click on the hamburger icon on the top left to open the navigational menu.
  • Here you will find different pages you can proceed with
  • If you click on Manage Appointments, you can see a list of appointments with each Service Provider
  • You can also Close an appointment from here. Also, you can see various details like Appointment details, Vehicle Details, Owner Details and Maintenance Records
  • Click on Manage Service Providers to manage your Providers
  • Here you can add a new Provider and edit/delete a provider if you want to.
  • Since you are a Super Admin, you can Add/Remove other Admins. Just check your menu
  • In case you want to come back to this page again, clicking on the Home button will do.
